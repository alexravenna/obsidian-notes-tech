	- audio
	- keyboard
	- mouse
	- joystick
	- graphics
- Officially supports:
	- [[Windows]]
	- [[macOS]]
	- [[Linux]]
	- [[iOS]]
	- [[Android]]
- Written in [[C]]
- Works natively with [[C++]] and has bindings for other languages
